# OpenClaw Desktop

Desktop application for OpenClaw.

Website: https://openclawdesktop.com/

OpenClaw Desktop (formerly **MoltBot / ClawdBot**) is **the AI that actually does things**—it runs locally on your computer and lets you control your PC via **WhatsApp, Telegram, or Discord**.

## Highlights

- **One-click installer**
- **Runs locally (privacy-first)** — your data stays on your device
- **Multi-platform chat control** — WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord
- **Works with your favorite AI** — Claude, GPT-4, or local AI via Ollama
- **Free & open-source**

## What it does

- **Email & calendar automation** (clear inbox, schedule meetings, send emails)
- **Browser automation** (fill forms, navigate sites, automate workflows)
- **File & system access** (with your permission)
- **Community skills (ClawHub)** — extend capabilities with community-built integrations

## How it works

1. **Download** the one-click installer
2. **Run** it and follow the prompts
3. **Connect your AI** (Claude / GPT-4 / Ollama)
4. **Control your PC** from your chat app
